BuddyCallAI, operated by Brash3D Media Group, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services in the United States and Canada.

  • Usage data: IP address, browser, pages visited, language preference

  • To calculate and pay affiliate commissions (5% Starter, 7% Pro, 9% Gold)
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, password reset, transcript delivery)
  • To comply with US and Canadian law
